His Story

Raised for Such a Time as This

Pastor Nehemiah Abraham is a man marked by a deep hunger for the presence of God and an unwavering commitment to His Word. Even before stepping into full-time ministry, he was wholly devoted to seeking the Lord with all his heart, soul, and mind. Alongside this pursuit, he excelled as an entrepreneur, building successful ventures that reflected wisdom, excellence, and integrity - demonstrating what it means to be led by the Spirit in every sphere of life.

In alignment with Zechariah 4:10, "Do not despise small beginnings," Nehemiah Abraham Ministries began as a simple yet divinely appointed assignment to reach a generation in Saket, New Delhi. What started small, through obedience to God's call, has grown into a thriving, Spirit-filled community of believers - young and old - hungry for the Lord.

Today, the ministry is headquartered in Saket, in the heart of India's capital, and continues to expand both in-person and online. Its reach now extends across India and into nations such as Dubai and Norway, raising a generation of believers who are rooted in the Word, established in faith, and walking in the reality of who they are in Christ.

Pastor Nehemiah carries a distinct grace to raise people who steward their gifts with wisdom and excellence and who boldly step into their God-given destiny. His ministry is marked by transformation - lives being changed, the sick being healed, and individuals encountering the power of God in tangible ways. Testimonies of supernatural provision, restoration, healing, and growth stand as evidence that the Gospel of Jesus Christ is still the power of God unto salvation.

At his core, Pastor Nehemiah Abraham is a man of prayer, a faithful servant of God, and a spiritual father committed to raising a generation that carries the presence of God into every sphere of life.

Purpose

Vision & Mission

The heartbeat behind everything Nehemiah Abraham Ministries does

Our Vision

  • Raising a generation rooted in the Word and filled with the Spirit, living with a Kingdom-first mindset in every area of life.
  • Igniting revival in hearts - where the Word is loved, faith is lived, and victory over the world is a daily reality.
  • Establishing believers in their identity, inheritance, and divine purpose in Christ Jesus.

Our Mission

  • Taking the message of the Word of Faith to the nations, equipping believers to live in alignment with God's higher ways of life.
  • Reaching the lost, the broken, and the needy - leading them into encounters with the God who heals, restores, and makes whole.
  • Empowering believers to take ownership of the Word, applying its promises and walking in daily dependence on God.
  • Raising mature sons and daughters who walk in authority, purpose, and the fullness of life in Christ.
Doctrine

What We Believe

Our ministry is founded on the timeless truths of God's Word.

The Word of God

We take God at His Word. His integrity is bound to His Word, and what He has spoken, He will surely perform. We live with confidence that His Word is final, unchanging, and absolutely trustworthy.

The Holy Spirit

We believe in the indwelling presence of the Holy Spirit and the baptism of the Spirit upon every believer. He empowers us to live a supernatural life, with expressions that include speaking in tongues and walking in the fullness of His power.

The Love of the Father

The Gospel reveals the heart of a good Father who loves us deeply. Through Jesus, we are brought into sonship - secure in His love, established in our identity, and invited into a life of relationship with Him.

Healing

Healing is the children's bread. God's desire to heal is unwavering - so much so that through Jesus, provision was already made. By His stripes, we were healed, and we live in the confidence of His finished work, receiving and walking in divine health.

Generosity & Kingdom Prosperity

We believe in prosperity with a Kingdom purpose. Jesus became poor so that we might be enriched - not just for ourselves, but to be a blessing. We are called to steward resources with wisdom, generosity, and a heart for advancing God's Kingdom.

The Salvation of Souls

God's heart is for the lost, and His desire is that none should perish. We carry this same burden - to reach the lost, proclaim the Gospel, and see lives transformed by the saving power of Jesus Christ.

Faith

Faith is not a feeling - it is a choice and a way of life. It is the spirit by which we live, believe, and speak. We are called to walk by faith in every area, trusting fully in who God is and what He has said.
