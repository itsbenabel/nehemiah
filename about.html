His Story

Raised Up for Such a Time as This

From the earliest days of his faith, Pastor Nehemiah Abraham knew he had been called to something beyond the ordinary. Born with a hunger for the presence of God, he encountered the prophetic anointing at a young age — an encounter that would shape the entire trajectory of his life and ministry.

Over more than a decade of faithful service, Pastor Nehemiah has built a ministry that stands on an uncompromising commitment to the Word of God. His preaching is marked by clarity, depth, and a burning prophetic edge that challenges believers to step into everything God has for them — not tomorrow, but today.

His heart for the nations has taken him across the globe — from India to Africa, from Southeast Asia to North America. Everywhere he goes, the same result follows: lives are transformed, the sick are healed, the bound are set free, and the lost are found. Pastor Nehemiah firmly believes that the Gospel of Jesus Christ is still the power of God unto salvation for every person who believes.

As a church planter, Pastor Nehemiah has a deep passion for establishing strong, Spirit-filled communities of believers who are rooted in the Word and empowered by the Holy Spirit. His vision is to see a generation of believers who do not merely attend church, but who carry the church — the presence of God — wherever they go.

As an author, Pastor Nehemiah has penned several books that have equipped thousands of believers in the areas of prophetic ministry, faith, and Kingdom living. His writing carries the same anointing as his preaching — it is not merely inspirational, it is transformational.

Above all, Pastor Nehemiah Abraham is a man of prayer, a servant of God, and a father in the faith — deeply committed to the people God has placed in his life and to the mission he has been entrusted with.

Purpose

Vision & Mission

The heartbeat behind everything Nehemiah Abraham Ministries does

Our Vision

Empowering every believer to walk in the fullness of God's purpose — living as carriers of His presence, vessels of His power, and ambassadors of His Kingdom in every sphere of society and every corner of the earth.

Our Mission

Proclaiming the uncompromised Word of God through preaching, teaching, prophetic ministry, and resources — equipping the saints for the work of the ministry and mobilising a generation to fulfil the Great Commission.

Doctrine

What We Believe

Our ministry is founded on the timeless truths of God's Word

The Word of God

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible, and authoritative Word of God — the final standard for all matters of faith and conduct.

Salvation

We believe in the death, burial, and resurrection of Jesus Christ, and that salvation comes by grace through faith in Him alone.

The Holy Spirit

We believe in the present ministry of the Holy Spirit, including the baptism of the Spirit and the operation of all His gifts in the Church today.

Healing

We believe in divine healing as provided in the atonement. Jesus Christ is the same yesterday, today, and forever — He still heals the sick.

Prophetic Ministry

We believe in the prophetic ministry as a gift to the Body of Christ — to edify, exhort, and comfort, and to reveal God's purposes for this generation.

The Church

We believe in the local and universal Church as the Body of Christ — called, equipped, and sent to represent God's Kingdom in the earth.
