Kingdom Law

The Law of Sowing & Reaping

God established this principle at creation and it has never been revoked.

You Must Sow to Reap

A seed that stays in your hand can never become a harvest. Giving is an act of faith — releasing what you have into the ground of God's purposes and trusting Him to multiply it.

“Cast your bread upon the waters, for after many days you will find it again.” — Ecclesiastes 11:1

You Reap What You Sow

God's harvest law is precise and cannot be manipulated. Sow into His Kingdom and expect Kingdom fruit in return. Generosity always attracts generosity.

“Do not be deceived: God cannot be mocked. A man reaps what he sows.” — Galatians 6:7

You Reap More Than You Sow

One seed becomes many. God does not return measure for measure — pressed down, shaken together, and running over is His minimum standard of return.

“Give, and it will be given to you — a good measure, pressed down, shaken together, running over.” — Luke 6:38
