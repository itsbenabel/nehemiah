Kingdom Law

The Law of Sowing & Reaping

God established this principle at creation and it has never been revoked.

Give, and It Will Be Given

We believe in the unchanging law of sowing and reaping. Giving is an act of faith and a partnership with God's Kingdom. As we sow into His purposes, we position ourselves for increase - because as His Kingdom grows, so do we.

The Law of Multiplication

Every harvest begins with a seed. What we sow determines what we reap, and God's pattern is always multiplication - thirty, sixty, and a hundredfold. Seed never leaves our life; it enters our future as a multiplied harvest.

Giving by Revelation

We give not out of obligation, but by revelation. God is King, and giving is our response of honour and worship. We trust His nature completely - knowing we can never outgive Him, for He always returns in abundance.
