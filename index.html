About Pastor Nehemiah
A Voice
to the
Nations

Pastor Nehemiah Abraham is a pastor, teacher, and author whose ministry has touched thousands of lives across the globe. With a burning passion for revival, a deep love for the Word of God, and a strong prophetic mantle, Pastor Nehemiah has been raised up for such a time as this.

Through years of faithful ministry, he has seen the transforming power of God at work in every nation his voice has reached - healing broken hearts, setting captives free, and calling believers into their divine purpose.
